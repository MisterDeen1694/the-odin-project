Zuppa Inglsas

Description

Dessert with brioche and vanilla custard in fifteen minutes. Then wait an hour. If you can.

Ingredients

  • 200 gram brioche loaves
  • 100 mililiter Fristi
  • 100 gram fresh respberries
  • 100 gram fresh red berries
  • 200 mililiter vanilla custard

Steps

  1. Cut the brioche into slices and cover the bottom of the glasses with it.
  2. Sprinkle the brioche with the raspberry juice.
  3. Divide the raspberries and red currants over it.
  4. Fill each glass with whipped cream custard.
  5. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
