Chocolate Apples

Description

A tasty combination of juicy apples and rich chocolate that will surprise your taste buds. A simple, sweet recipe to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 20 apples
  • 300 gram chocolate
  • 200 gram sprinkles of your choice
  • 1 baking paper

Steps

  1. Scrub the apples clean.
  2. Heat the chocolate according to the instructions on the package.
  3. Dip the bottom of an apple in the chocolate and let it drain. Wait 20 sec. and then dip in the sprinkles.
  4. lace the dipped apples on baking paper until the chocolate has hardened.
