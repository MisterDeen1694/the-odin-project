Limoncello cheesecake

Description

What could possibly be better than a glass of limoncello? Limoncello cheesecake! With a base of cantuccini cookies and decorated with lemon slices, this cake is a treat that no one can resist.

Ingredients

  • 200 gram cantuccini
  • 80 gram unsalted butter
  • 4 leaves gelatin
  • 600 gram fresh cream cheese
  • 45 gram granulated suger
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 2 lemons
  • 250 mililiter frech cream
  • 100 mililiter Limoncello

Steps

  1. Grease the springform pan and line the bottom with baking paper. Grind the cantuccini finely in the food processor. Melt the butter. Place the crushed cookies in a bowl and stir in the butter. Spread the mixture evenly over the bottom of the mold and press firmly. Place in the refrigerator until use.
  2. Soak the gelatin leaves in cold water for 5 minutes. In the meantime, stir the cream cheese with the sugar and vanilla extract with a whisk until you have a smooth mixture. Scrub the lemons clean, grate the yellow zest of 1½ lemons and squeeze 1½ lemons (per pie). Add the grater to the cream cheese.
  3. Place 60 ml lemon juice (per cake) in a saucepan and heat over low heat. Remove the pan from the heat. Squeeze the gelatin and mix with the lemon juice until the gelatin is completely dissolved. Let cool slightly.
  4. In the meantime, beat the whipped cream with a mixer until stiff. Add the gelatin mixture and limoncello to the cream cheese and mix well. Carefully fold in the whipped cream. Spread the filling over the base and smooth out. Place the cheesecake covered in the refrigerator for at least 3 hours.
  5. Cut the remaining lemon into thin slices and divide over the cheesecake before serving.
