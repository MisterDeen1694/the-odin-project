Jack-o'-Lantern Jelly Jars

Trick or treat yourself to these boo-tiful fruity desserts. Happy haunting!

Ingredients

  • 45 g orange jelly crystals
  • 1 L boiling water
  • 1 L cold water
  • 2 mangoes
  • 4 peaches
  • 1 bunch mint

Steps

  1. Using a permanent marker, draw jack-o'-lantern faces onto each mason jar.
  2. Cut peaches into slices, and dice the mangoes into cubes.
  3. Pour orange jelly crystals into a large bowl, then mix with 1 litre of boiling water. Stir until the crystals have dissolved completely, then add 1 litre of cold water. Stir the liquid for 5 minutes and then place into a pouring jug.
  4. Divide diced fruit equally among mason jars, and then fill each jar with the orange jelly liquid. Screw on the lid and set in the fridge for at least 8 hours.
  5. When jelly has set, decorate with a sprig of mint.
